BIOGRAPHY

I am a research scientist in the AI team at the Cardiovascular Center of Seoul National University Bundang Hospital, working under a contractual agreement with medical doctor Si-Hyuk Kang. I earned my M.S. from the Advanced Application for Intelligence Systems Laboratory, where I studied deep learning theory and explored novel learning approaches under the guidance of Professor Young-kyun Noh. My thesis proposes novel learning methods for handling censored data in survival analysis, along with a new objective function designed to directly optimize classification metrics in particle physics. I am interested fundamentally in the theoretical issues in machine learning for large-scale data analysis. Currently, I focus on machine learning methods for practical applications in clinical settings.
