I am a research scientist on the AI team at the Cardiovascular Center of Seoul National University Bundang Hospital, contracted by professor M.D. Kang.¬† I earned my M.S. from the Advanced Application for Intelligence Systems Laboratory, where I studied machine learning algorithms under Yung-Kyun Noh. My thesis proposes novel learning methods for censored data in survival analysis and a new objective function for directly maximizing classification learning metrics in particle physics. I am interested fundamentally in the theoretical issues in machine learning for large-scale data analysis. Currently, I focus on machine learning methods for practical applications in clinical settings.
